Grokking Tech

Resources for Understanding Technology: Videos | Conferences

What?

Grokking Tech is a collection of resources for learning about technology, with an emphasis on Computer Sciense and Information Security. Currently, the content consists of video recordings from conferences.

Browse the list of videos, or the list of conferences.

Grokking Tech is maintained on GitHub.
